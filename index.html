Meet Evan Nixon

I've been helping North Alabama families, farmers, and business owners protect what matters since joining Hayes Rasbury Agency, Inc. in 2018. I specialize in personal, farm, commercial, and life insurance out of our Decatur and Birmingham offices.

Insurance can be confusing — my job is to make it simple. I take the time to explain your options clearly, shop multiple carriers for the best value, and I'm there for you when it counts: during claims, renewals, and everything in between.

Born and raised in Albertville, AL, I live here with my wife Hallie, our son Easton, and our German Shepherd, Kimber. When I'm not working, you'll find me duck hunting, shooting skeet, or on the golf course.
